SAMPLE SERVICE AGREEMENT

This Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into on [Date] between ABC Corporation ("Client") and XYZ Services ("Provider").

1. SERVICES
Provider shall provide consulting services to Client with reasonable best efforts. The services shall include project management, technical consulting, and strategic guidance.

2. PAYMENT TERMS
Client agrees to pay Provider the sum of $50,000 for services rendered. Payment shall be made within 30 days of invoice date. Late payments may incur a penalty of 1.5% per month.

3. LIABILITY AND INDEMNIFICATION
Provider shall indemnify and hold harmless Client against all claims, damages, and expenses arising from Provider's performance. Client's liability is unlimited for any breach of confidentiality obligations.

4. TERMINATION
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. Upon termination for convenience by Client, Provider shall be entitled to liquidated damages equal to 50% of the remaining contract value.

5. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY
All work product, including proprietary information and trade secrets developed under this agreement, shall be assigned to Client as work for hire. Provider waives all moral rights in such work.

6. CONFIDENTIALITY
Both parties agree to maintain confidential information in strictest confidence. This obligation shall survive termination and continue indefinitely.

7. GOVERNING LAW
This agreement shall be governed by the laws of Delaware. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Delaware.

8. NON-COMPETE
Provider agrees not to compete with Client's business for a period of 2 years following termination in any geographic market where Client operates.

9. FORCE MAJEURE
Neither party shall be liable for delays due to force majeure events including acts of God, natural disasters, or government actions.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Agreement.
